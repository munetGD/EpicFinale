The Saxon

Meet Saxon!

Saxon is a teenage explorer who, whilst navigating PsychologyLand, is trying to understand who is and where he belongs, like many people tend to do during adolescence.

Saxon represents the literal process of becoming and growing, reminding all of our visitors that knowledge and improvement are all about the journey.

"I don't know everything, but I can learn about who I am becoming." - The Saxon

Personality and Behavior:

Saxon is curious, emotional, reflective, and occasionally uncertain.

He inquires, experiments with new experiences, and occasionally second-guesses himself.

He learns best through exploration and feedback. He grows from his success and his failure.

Saxon commonly pauses to reflect, or seek advice from others. This shows that development can occur through social interaction and self-examination.
